## Data-Backed Design Decisions

These surveys directly informed Cherri's product design:

1. **Wide gusset design**: 97% said standard gussets are too small
2. **Inclusive sizing (XS-4X)**: Body diversity data showed need
3. **Breathable organic cotton**: 56% experienced infections from non-breathable materials
4. **"Outie-friendly" design**: 31-46% have outie vulvas, yet most brands ignore this

## Usage Guidelines

- These statistics can be used in marketing and content
- Round to whole percentages for clarity (e.g., "97%" not "97.3%")
- When citing, reference "survey of 5,000+ people" or "survey of 860+ people"
- For emotional stories, use paraphrased versions to protect privacy
