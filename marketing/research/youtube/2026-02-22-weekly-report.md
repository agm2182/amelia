## Executive Summary

- **YouTube underwear content is sparse and niche.** Of 176 raw videos from 12 searches, only 10 were uploaded in the last 90 days. YouTube's relevance algorithm surfaces older popular content, making weekly monitoring harder than Reddit. The most relevant underwear content comes from small creators (< 500 subs) who aren't gaming the algorithm.
- **Organic cotton underwear is getting genuine reviews.** Two small creators reviewed organic cotton underwear products (Danish Endurance, SHARICCA) with authentic, detail-oriented content — exactly the audience Cherri targets. These creators have high engagement relative to their subscriber counts.
- **Skims is extending into activewear via the NikeSkims collab** — a mid-size creator (20.6K subs) covered the launch with a positive haul. This signals Skims broadening beyond intimates into athleisure.
- **MeUndies is sending PR to micro-influencers.** A 34-sub creator received a MeUndies PR package, suggesting MeUndies is investing in micro-influencer seeding. Notably, the only comment asks about gusset width — a Cherri differentiator MeUndies doesn't address.
- **The biggest content gap is mid-funnel underwear comparison content.** No recent videos compare everyday underwear brands head-to-head. Tier lists and brand rankings are all 6+ months old. This is a content opportunity for Cherri.

The creator specifically chose organic cotton because she "always looks for cotton."

- **Unflattering fits for curvy bodies** — Blogilates denim review comments discuss thigh fit issues and "waist gaping" problems. Top comment (1,100 likes): "As a fellow curvy jean girlie I've been so skeptical."

### What People Wish Existed

- **Gusset and fit details in underwear reviews** — The only comment on the MeUndies review asks: "How do they fit? How do they feel when working out? How is the gusset? What is the fabric?" These are exactly the questions Cherri's product pages should answer.
- **Head-to-head brand comparisons** — No recent video (< 90 days) compares everyday underwear brands. All tier list and ranking videos are 6+ months old. Viewers searching for "best underwear brands 2026" find stale content.
- **Size-inclusive underwear reviews** — Despite searching for plus-size underwear content, no recent video from a creator with meaningful reach addressed this topic.
