### Filter Analysis

The aggressive funnel (176 → 2) reveals structural characteristics of YouTube's underwear content landscape:

- **Age filter removed 93% of videos** (150 → 10). Most underwear review content on YouTube is old. Creators are not producing new underwear-specific content at high volume — the format has shifted to TikTok and Instagram.
- **NSFW filter removed 40% of remaining** (10 → 6). "Underwear" on YouTube is heavily polluted by explicit try-on haul content. Any Cherri YouTube strategy must carefully title/tag videos to avoid being grouped with NSFW content.
- **Spam filter removed 67% of remaining** (6 → 2). The most relevant niche content (organic cotton reviews, seamless underwear) comes from very small channels (<500 subs). These micro-creators are the authentic voice in this space.

**Recommendation:** Future sweeps may benefit from lowering the spam threshold (e.g., 100 subs) for this niche, since small creators produce the most relevant underwear content on YouTube.
