# Cherri Competitor Intelligence

Last updated: January 2025

## Overview

This document captures competitive intelligence for Cherri's underwear brand. Competitors are segmented into **Direct** (similar positioning/scale) and **Aspirational** (larger brands to learn from).

---

These brands compete in similar categories with comparable positioning or target similar customer segments.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|

| **Scale** | $20M investment raised (2025) |
| **Unique Value** | Functional health story + proprietary materials |

**Research Questions:**
