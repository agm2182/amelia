# Panty Drop Competitive Deep Dive

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Executive Summary

Panty Drop is a plus-size focused underwear brand founded by MIT graduate Julie Arsenault in 2016. The brand partnered with Dr. Deborah Christel (Ph.D. in apparel design, women's studies, and fat studies) to create truly size-inclusive underwear from XS to 8X using 3D pattern-making methodology rather than scaled-up small sizes.

## 1. Unique Value Prop: 3D Pattern-Making

**What They Claim:**

- **Traditional brands:** Take a 2D pattern (usually size small) and scale it up uniformly
- **Panty Drop approach:** "Work in 3 dimensions" and "adjust dozens of measurements to create the perfect fit at every size"

**Specific Adjustments Per Size:**
- Rise height optimized (larger sizes have higher rises)
- Gusset width increases proportionally
- Back coverage scales independently
- Leg openings widen for larger thighs
- Waistband construction varies by size

**Development Process:**
Dr. Deb Christel combines academic research (surveyed thousands of plus-size customers, consulted with post-bariatric clinics) with direct feedback:
1. Pattern drafting based on customer needs
2. Sample creation at factory
3. Fit testing with multiple body types
4. "Micro-revisions" to perfect each size

**Key Messaging:** "We didn't just stretch out a size small. We made hundreds of micro-adjustments to each size individually."

## 2. How They Handle XS-8X Sizing Profitably

**Business Model Evolution:**
- Started 2016 as multi-brand subscription curator
- Developed proprietary "Perfect Panty" line
- Now focuses primarily on own products

**Profitability Factors:**
1. **Direct-to-consumer only** - Higher margins
2. **Limited SKU count** - One hero product in multiple colors
3. **Subscription model** - Predictable recurring revenue
4. **Mystery color options** - Moves excess inventory at $12.80/pair (vs. $18 regular)
5. **Bundle pricing** - Incentivizes multi-pair purchases

**Challenges:**
- Extended sizing (8X) requires more fabric
- Custom patterns = higher development costs
- Long-tail inventory issues in extreme sizes

## 4. Why Customers Say "Life Changing"

**Key Pain Points Solved:**

1. **"Lip Slip" Prevention**
   - Wide gusset prevents "being half-in, half-out"
   - "Tired of trying to fit all your bits in a tiny strip of fabric?"

2. **Stay-Put Waistband**
   - Doesn't roll down under belly
   - Sits "just below belly button"

3. **Full Tummy Coverage**
   - Minimizes skin-on-skin contact
   - Reduces chafing and under-belly sweat

4. **No Leg Elastic Dig**

5. **Proper Rise at Each Size**
