# April 2026 Rebrand Launch Strategy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Executive Summary

Cherri's April rebrand represents a strategic repositioning: same beloved brand/product, new $18 price point (down from $28), and refreshed  visual identity. With inventory arriving in April and Parade's exit creating market vacuum, this launch has significant upside potential.

---

## 1. Launch Context

### What's Changing
| Element | Before | After |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Price | $28/pair | $18/pair |
| Logo | Previous version | New GenZ-friendly design |
| Target Demo | Broad | Refined based on market research |
| Positioning | Comfort-focused | "Underwear that actually fits" |

### What's NOT Changing
- Brand name: Cherri
- Product: Same quality, same gusset innovation
- Voice: Cheeky, witty, playful, body-positive
- Mission: Solving the fit problem no one talks about

### Market Timing Advantages
1. **Parade Exit:** $60M+ funded competitor closed October 2025 - gap in affordable-premium, inclusive, sustainable underwear
2. **Spring Shopping:** April = wardrobe refresh mentality
3. **Pre-Summer:** Timing before beach/vacation season
4. **Tax Refund Season:** Discretionary spending window

## 2. Pre-Launch Phase (February - March)

### 2.1 Email List Growth (6 weeks out)

**Goal:** Add 2,000+ new subscribers before launch

**Tactics:**
1. **Lead Magnet:** "The Ultimate Underwear Fit Guide" PDF
   - What to measure
   - Common fit problems solved
   - Style guide by body type

2. **Exit-Intent Popup:** Capture visitors leaving site
   - "Don't miss our April rebrand - big changes coming"

3. **Social Cross-Promotion:**
   - IG Stories with "Link in bio" CTA
   - TikTok content pointing to signup

4. **Waitlist for New Price:**
   - "Be first to shop at our new price point"
   - Creates anticipation and FOMO

### 2.2 Content Pipeline Building (6 weeks out)

**Content Bank to Create:**

| Content Type | Quantity | Purpose |
|--------------|----------|---------|
| TikTok videos | 20+ | Launch week + ongoing |
| IG Reels | 15+ | Cross-posted + native |
| IG Stories templates | 10 | Polls, countdowns, reveals |
| Email sequence | 5 emails | Welcome + launch series |
| UGC examples | 10+ | Social proof |

**Content Themes:**
1. **Teaser Content:** "Something's coming..."
2. **Problem-Aware:** Gusset/fit education
3. **Founder Story:** Why Cherri exists
4. **Behind-the-Scenes:** Rebrand process
5. **Customer Stories:** Real testimonials

### 2.3 Campus Ambassador Seeding (6 weeks out)

**Target:** 5-10 ambassadors at 1-2 schools

**Priority Schools:**
- SEC schools with strong Greek life: Alabama, Ole Miss, Georgia
- Fashion-forward: UT Austin, UCLA, USC

**Ambassador Incentive Structure:**
| Tier | Requirements | Rewards |
|------|--------------|---------|
| Starter | Join program | 1 free item, 15% personal code, 10% follower code |
| Rising Star | 3+ posts/month, $200+ sales | Extra product, 12% commission |
| Ambassador | 5+ posts/month, $500+ sales | Monthly box, 15% commission |

**Timeline:**
- Week 1-2: Identify and reach out
- Week 3-4: Onboard first cohort
- Week 5-6: Seed product, train on brand voice

### 2.4 Influencer/Affiliate Outreach (4 weeks out)

**Micro-Influencer Targets:**
- Female comedians (TikTok)
- Body-positive fashion creators (IG)
- Neurodivergent creators discussing sensory issues
- "Relatable girl" lifestyle creators

**Outreach Volume:** 50-100 DMs/emails

**Pitch:**
> "Hey [Name]! I'm Gabriella, founder of Cherri - we make underwear with a wider gusset that actually fits (no more lip slips). Your content about [specific reference] resonated with me. Would you be interested in trying our product? Launching a rebrand in April with a new $18 price point."

**Seed Product Budget:** $500-1,000 (25-50 units)

### 2.5 Reddit Community Warming (Ongoing)

**Authentic Engagement Strategy:**
- Participate genuinely in r/femalefashionadvice, r/ABraThatFits
- Answer underwear questions without promoting
- Build karma and trust before launch
- Don't spam - be helpful

**When Asked Directly:** Can mention Cherri if contextually relevant

## 4. Messaging Framework

### Primary Message
**"Same Cherri. New Price. Better Fit."**

Supporting messages:
- "Underwear that actually fits - now at $18"
- "No lip slips. No compromises."
- "The rebrand you've been waiting for"

### Price Drop Messaging
**DO:** Frame as accessibility, not desperation
- "We wanted more people to experience real comfort"
- "Premium fit shouldn't mean premium price"

**DON'T:**
- "We lowered our prices!" (sounds desperate)
- Compare directly to old price
- Over-explain the decision

### Parade Refugee Messaging
**Subtle, not direct attack:**
- "Looking for your new go-to?"
- "Miss that brand that closed? We get it."
- Don't mention Parade by name publicly

### Differentiation Messages
1. **Fit:** "The only underwear designed for how you're actually built"
2. **Gusset:** "Wide gusset = no more front wedgies"
3. **Quality:** "Premium supima cotton at an accessible price"
4. **Female-Founded:** "Built by a woman who was tired of uncomfortable underwear"

## 8. Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Launch Falls Flat
**Mitigation:**
- Build pre-launch hype to guarantee day-one traffic
- Have backup content ready to pivot messaging
- Plan "phase 2" push for week 2 if needed

### Risk: Inventory Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Confirm April arrival dates by March 15
- Have backup messaging if delayed
- Consider pre-order model if needed

### Risk: Price Perception
**Mitigation:**
- Emphasize quality maintained, not reduced
- Lead with value, not discount
- Testimonials about quality

### Risk: Low Influencer Response
**Mitigation:**
- Start outreach early (6+ weeks)
- Volume approach (50-100 contacts)
- Have ambassador program as backup
