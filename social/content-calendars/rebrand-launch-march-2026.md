## Filming & Asset Notes

**No new product photography exists.** Every entry is tagged with what's needed:
- **Talking Head (TH):** Gaby on camera, no product needed
- **Existing Product (EP):** Use current product shots/footage
- **New Branding (NB):** Requires new logo/visual assets (digital, not photography)
- **Screen/Text (ST):** Text overlay, screenshot, or designed graphic

**Batch filming rec:** Shoot TH content for March 23-30 in one session (~8-10 clips). Change tops between takes for variety. Shoot April 1-6 TH content in a second session.
