## Content Pillars

Reference from [brand-voice-positioning.md](../../brand/brand-voice-positioning.md):

| Pillar | Description | % of Weekly Mix | Example Topics |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Educational** | Fit tips, gusset explainers, fabric info, underwear myths | 40% | "Why gusset width matters," "How to find your size," "Supima cotton vs. regular cotton" |
| **Entertaining** | Relatable humor, trending sounds, memes, dating/body comedy | 30% | "Things only wide-gusset girlies understand," trending sound adaptations, "rating my underwear drawer" |
| **Product** | Try-ons, new drops, restocks, styling, bundles, TikTok Shop | 20% | Unboxing, "what's in the bundle," color launches, flash sales |
| **Founder/BTS** | Origin story, day-in-the-life, brand decisions, team content | 10% | "Why I started Cherri," packing orders, design process, Amelia takeovers |

### Voice Quick Reference
- **Cheeky, direct, relatable, playful, not try-hard**
- TikTok = raw, humor-first, trending sounds, irreverent
- Instagram = curated, editorial, Pinterest-y cool girl who also makes coochie jokes
- Language that works: "lip slips," "vajussy," "fruitcake," "flaps," holiday/seasonal hooks, dating and body humor
- Avoid: body positivity lectures, clinical language, over-explanation, "boss babe" energy, forced enthusiasm

## Batch Filming Checklist

Use this when batch filming for the week:

**Pre-Filming:**
- [ ] All hooks/scripts written for the week
- [ ] 4-5 outfit changes ready
- [ ] Ring light / natural light setup
- [ ] Phone charged, storage cleared
- [ ] Props if needed (product, packaging, laptop for reaction content)

**Filming Order** (saves energy):
1. Educational content first (calm, focused delivery)
2. Founder/BTS content (personal, reflective)
3. Product content (needs product in hand)
4. Humor/trending content last (highest energy needed)

**Post-Filming:**
- [ ] Transfer to editing device
- [ ] Edit and caption
- [ ] Schedule or save as drafts
- [ ] Cross-post TikToks to Reels (re-edit for native feel where possible)
