## EDITING NOTES

- **Existing footage first:** Build the edit around the b-roll you already have. The confessionals are the glue between clips.
- **Keep the model out of frame.** Only show hands, fabric, the computer screen, and Gaby/Amelia.
- **Nipple clamps clip:** Use the real audio from the existing clip. Don't re-record it — the spontaneity is the joke.
- **Silly outfit:** Don't telegraph it. Hard cut to Gaby already wearing it. No buildup.
- **Cherries:** Same energy — they just appear. No explanation in the footage, only in confessionals.
- **Pacing:** Fast cuts for the work montage (Scene 4). Let the confessionals breathe — the pauses and stares are where the comedy lives.
- **Fade to title:** Same style as ep 2 — gaussian blur, clean text, 2–3 seconds.
- **Captions:** Caption ALL dialogue. Most viewers watch muted.
