## EDITING NOTES

- **Pacing:** Fast cuts between confessionals and action, like The Office. Don't let any single shot sit for more than 3–4 seconds (exception: the lunch silence).
- **Zoom-ins:** Use slow awkward zoom-ins on people's faces when they react to something — Jim Halpert looking at the camera energy. Add these in post on any static shots.
- **Lunch silence:** Let it sit. The discomfort IS the comedy. Minimum 3 seconds of pure dead air.
- **Ending sequence:** The last 3 beats should flow quickly: Gaby's "great boss" confessional → model's "free underwear" → fade/blur to title card. Boom boom boom. Don't linger between them.
- **Fade to title:** Gaussian blur or soft fade on the last shot, then clean text: `cherri launching soon`. Keep the title card for 2–3 seconds. Optionally add `Cherri HQ: Behind The Seams` smaller underneath.
- **Audio:** Dialogue and confessionals carry the video. Subtle background track is fine but not required. No trending audio needed unless one fits perfectly.
- **Captions/subtitles:** Caption ALL dialogue. Most viewers watch muted. Use a clean, readable font. White with black outline or a text box.
- **Text overlays:** Series title at the opening (Scene 1C), `cherri launching soon` title card at the end (Scene 13).
- **Color grading:** Keep it warm, bright, pretty. Match the premium feel of the actual ecom shoot where possible.
